About

Hi, I am a student at Manchester Codes. I have been learning CLI, git-hub, HTML, CSS and Javascript. I also have past experience with FORTRAN and C++. Ever since I started school I have loved maths. Throughout my education it has always been a strong focus of mine. I was first introduced to coding during my A-Level in computing, but it wasn't until university that I developed a passion for it. When I started coding at University in my undergraduate degree in maths I learnt how to program in FORTRAN. I wrote a program for my 4th year project to solve the Lorenz Equations. When I did my Masters in fluid dynamics I continued to write in FORTRAN for many projects. For my dissertation I taught myself C++ and wrote a program to track diffusion across two different fluids. I went on to become a maths teacher in secondary schools but I am now ready for a new challenge and want to become a software engineer. Manchester codes has reignited my passion for coding.
