About

Ever since I started school I have loved maths. Throughout my education it has always been a strong focus of mine. For my dissertation I taught myself C++ and wrote a program to track diffusion across two different fluids. I went on to become a maths teacher in secondary schools but I am now ready for a new challenge and want to become a software engineer. Manchester codes has reignited my passion for coding. So far I have been learning CLI, GitHub, HTML, CSS and Javascript.
